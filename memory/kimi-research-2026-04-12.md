## Key Findings

### Protocol Landscape
- **x402**: 100M+ cumulative txs, ~$28K/day real volume. $7B ecosystem valuation. 50-95% of volume possibly testing/gamed. Foundation members: Google, Visa, AWS, Anthropic, Circle, Vercel.
- **Virtuals ACP**: $479M "agentic GDP", $60M+ real revenue. $1M/month incentive program for active sellers. VIRTUAL token down 87% from ATH ($0.63 vs $5.07).
- **Eliza (AI16Z)**: Most-starred AI agent repo on GitHub. ~$1B market cap. Revenue model still evolving (voluntary token contributions, launchpad fees).
- **Bittensor (TAO)**: $3.2-3.5B market cap. 120+ subnets. Chutes ranks top on OpenRouter. Complex for individual builders.
- **Skyfire**: Agent wallet abstraction, KYA verification. Enterprise-focused, centralized.
- **Nevermined**: Protocol-native A2A/MCP/x402/AP2 support. Best compliance story.
- **Stripe MPP**: Launched March 2026. Fiat-first, may limit crypto-native adoption.

### Traction vs Hype
- Real: Virtuals $60M+ revenue, Bittensor subnet revenue, x402 institutional backing
- Hype: 90% of agent tokens have no revenue, 63% unaudited contracts, 200x P/S ratios
- **Key insight**: Infrastructure exists, demand doesn't. Supply-heavy market.

### The Gap: Payment → Profit
Missing pieces:
- Persistent agent identity/reputation
- Service quality assurance/SLA enforcement
- Discovery layer (how buyers find agents)
- Customer acquisition beyond protocol-native
